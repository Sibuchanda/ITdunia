Accenture

About Company: Accenture is a global professional services company with expertise in digital, cloud, and security solutions. It offers technology consulting, business strategy, and outsourcing services across various industries worldwide.

CEO: Julie Sweet

Headquarters: Dublin, Ireland

Founded: 1989

Industry: IT Services, Consulting & Outsourcing

Eligibility Criteria

  • B.E / B.Tech / M.E / M.Tech in Computer Science, IT, or related fields
  • MCA / M.Sc in Computer Science or IT
  • Minimum 60% aggregate in 10th, 12th, and Graduation
  • No active backlogs
  • Strong problem-solving and analytical skills

Placement Process

  1. Cognitive & Technical Assessment (Aptitude, Logical Reasoning, and Coding)
  2. Communication Assessment (Verbal, Spoken, and Writing Skills Test)
  3. Technical Interview
  4. HR Interview
  5. Final Offer

Hiring Exam

Accenture conducts the Accenture Hiring Assessment (AHA) to evaluate candidates on cognitive ability, problem-solving, and technical skills.
