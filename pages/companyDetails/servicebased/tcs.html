Tata Consultancy Services (TCS)

About Company: Tata Consultancy Services (TCS) is a global IT services, consulting, and business solutions provider. It offers services in software development, cloud computing, cybersecurity, and AI solutions.

CEO: K. Krithivasan

Headquarters: Mumbai, India

Founded: 1968

Industry: IT Services & Consulting

Eligibility Criteria

  • B.E / B.Tech / M.E / M.Tech / MCA / M.Sc in Computer Science or IT
  • Minimum 60% in 10th, 12th, and Graduation
  • No more than 1 backlog at the time of application
  • Good communication and problem-solving skills

Placement Process

  1. Online Assessment (Aptitude + Reasoning + Verbal + Coding)
  2. Technical Interview
  3. HR Interview
  4. Final Offer

Hiring Exam

TCS recruits freshers through its official TCS National Qualifier Test (TCS NQT), which assesses candidates on aptitude, reasoning, verbal ability, and coding skills.
