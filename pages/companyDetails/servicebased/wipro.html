Wipro

About Company: Wipro Limited is a leading global information technology, consulting, and business process services company. They harness the power of cognitive computing, hyper-automation, robotics, cloud, analytics, and emerging technologies to help clients adapt to the digital world.

CEO: Thierry Delaporte

Headquarters: Bangalore, India

Founded: 1945

Industry: IT Services & Consulting

Eligibility Criteria

  • BE/B.Tech (Compulsory Degree)/ME/M.Tech
  • MCA/MSc (Computer Science)
  • 60% or above in 10th and 12th standard
  • 65% or above in graduation
  • No active backlogs

Placement Process

  1. Aptitude Test
  2. Written Communication Test
  3. Online Programming Test
  4. Technical Interview
  5. HR Interview

Hiring Exam

Wipro conducts the Elite National Talent Hunt (NTH), a fresher's hiring program to recruit the best talent from engineering graduates across the country.
