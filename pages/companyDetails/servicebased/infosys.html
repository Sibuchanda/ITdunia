Infosys

About Company: Infosys is a global leader in next-generation digital services and consulting. It enables clients in over 56 countries to navigate their digital transformation. With over four decades of experience in managing the systems and workings of global enterprises, Infosys expertly steers clients through their digital journey.

CEO: Salil Parekh

Headquarters: Bangalore, India

Founded: 1981

Industry: IT Services & Consulting

Eligibility Criteria

  • B.E / B.Tech / M.E / M.Tech / MCA / M.Sc in Computer Science or IT
  • Minimum 60% in 10th, 12th, and Graduation
  • No active backlogs during application
  • Strong analytical and problem-solving skills

Placement Process

  1. Online Assessment (Aptitude, Logical Reasoning, Verbal Ability, and Coding)
  2. Technical Interview
  3. HR Interview
  4. Final Selection

Hiring Exam

Infosys conducts the Infosys Online Test as part of its recruitment process, assessing candidates on various competencies including aptitude and technical skills.
