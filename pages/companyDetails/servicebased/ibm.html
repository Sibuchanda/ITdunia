IBM

About Company: IBM (International Business Machines Corporation) is a global technology company providing hardware, software, cloud computing, AI, and consulting services to enterprises worldwide.

CEO: Arvind Krishna

Headquarters: Armonk, New York, USA

Founded: 1911

Industry: IT Services & Cloud Computing

Eligibility Criteria

  • B.E / B.Tech / M.E / M.Tech in Computer Science, IT, or related fields
  • MCA / M.Sc in Computer Science or IT
  • Minimum 65% in 10th, 12th, and Graduation
  • No active backlogs
  • Good problem-solving, coding, and communication skills

Placement Process

  1. Online Aptitude & Cognitive Assessment
  2. Coding Test (Technical Skills Assessment)
  3. Technical Interview
  4. HR Interview
  5. Final Offer

Hiring Exam

IBM conducts the **IBM Cognitive Ability & Technical Assessment (CAT)** to evaluate candidates on problem-solving, coding, and technical skills.
