Rapido

About Company: Rapido is India's largest bike taxi service, offering app-based two-wheeler ride-sharing, auto-rickshaw hailing, and delivery services. Founded in 2015 and headquartered in Bangalore, Rapido operates in over 100 cities across India. The platform aims to revolutionize intra-city travel by providing affordable and convenient commuting options.

Founders: Aravind Sanka, Pavan Guntupalli, and SR Rishikesh

Headquarters: Bangalore, Karnataka, India

Founded: 2015

Industry: Transportation, Ride-Hailing

Number of Employees: Approximately 3,428 (as of 2024)

Services: Bike taxi, Auto rickshaw hailing, Delivery services

Website: www.rapido.bike
