Byju's

About Company: Byju's is an Indian multinational educational technology company headquartered in Bangalore, Karnataka, India. Founded in 2011 by Byju Raveendran and Divya Gokulnath, the company offers online learning programs for students. Byju's has expanded its product offerings to include various subsidiaries such as Osmo, WhiteHat Jr, and Aakash Institute.

Founders: Byju Raveendran and Divya Gokulnath

Headquarters: Bangalore, Karnataka, India

Founded: 2011

Industry: Educational Technology (EdTech)

Number of Employees: Approximately 50,000

Funding: Byju's has raised a total funding of $4.45 billion over 27 rounds from 135 investors.
