Meesho

About Company: Meesho is an Indian social commerce platform that enables individuals and small businesses to start their online stores via social channels like WhatsApp, Facebook, and Instagram. Founded in 2015 by Vidit Aatrey and Sanjeev Barnwal, Meesho aims to democratize internet commerce by bringing a wide range of products to consumers at affordable prices.

CEO: Vidit Aatrey

Headquarters: Bangalore, Karnataka, India

Founded: 2015

Industry: E-commerce, Social Commerce

Number of Employees: Approximately 1,000-5,000

Funding: Meesho has raised significant funding, achieving unicorn status with a valuation exceeding $1 billion.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Bachelor’s or Master’s degree in Business, Marketing, Computer Science, or related fields
  • Experience in e-commerce, retail, or related industries
  • Strong analytical and problem-solving skills
  • Excellent communication and teamwork abilities

Placement Process

  1. Online Application

  4. HR Interview (Behavioral and Cultural Fit)
  5. Final Offer

Hiring Exam

Meesho conducts assessments focusing on domain knowledge, analytical abilities, and problem-solving skills relevant to the position applied for.
