Nykaa

About Company: Nykaa is an Indian retail company specializing in beauty, wellness, and fashion products. Founded in 2012 by Falguni Nayar, it has grown into one of India's largest cosmetics and lifestyle brands, offering a wide range of products through its website, mobile app, and over 100 physical stores. In 2020, Nykaa became the first Indian unicorn startup headed by a woman. The company's vision is to bring inspiration and joy to people, everywhere, every day.

CEO: Falguni Nayar

Headquarters: Mumbai, Maharashtra, India

Founded: 2012

Industry: E-commerce, Beauty, Fashion

Number of Employees: 2,000+ (as of 2021)

Ownership: Nayar family (53.5%)
