Shiprocket

About Company: Shiprocket is India's leading eCommerce logistics and shipping software solution, enabling eCommerce sellers with AI-driven technology and dependable services encompassing conversion, order fulfillment, shipping, buyer communication, returns management, and more. The platform leverages data, workflows, and supply chains at scale, providing users with comprehensive tools to enhance customer experience.

Founded: 2017

Industry: E-commerce Logistics

Services: Shipping, Order Fulfillment, Returns Management

Website: www.shiprocket.in
