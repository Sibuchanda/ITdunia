Ola

About Company: Ola, officially known as Ola Consumer, is India's largest mobility platform and one of the world's largest ride-hailing companies. Founded in 2010 by Bhavish Aggarwal and Ankit Bhati, Ola offers a range of mobility solutions, including ride-hailing services across bikes, auto-rickshaws, metered taxis, and cabs. The company operates in over 250 cities across India, Australia, New Zealand, and the UK. In addition to its core transportation services, Ola has diversified into areas such as financial services and food delivery.
