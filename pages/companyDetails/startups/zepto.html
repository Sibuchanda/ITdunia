Zepto

About Company: Zepto is an Indian quick commerce startup founded in 2021 by Aadit Palicha and Kaivalya Vohra. The company specializes in delivering a wide range of products, including fresh groceries, electronics, beauty essentials, apparel, and toys, within 10 minutes across 50+ cities in India. Zepto leverages cutting-edge technology and strategically optimized delivery hubs to revolutionize rapid commerce in the country.

Founders: Aadit Palicha and Kaivalya Vohra

Headquarters: Bangalore, Karnataka, India

Founded: 2021

Industry: Quick Commerce, E-commerce

Number of Employees: Approximately 1,000-5,000

Funding: Zepto has raised significant funding, including a $350 million round in November 2024, maintaining its valuation at $5 billion.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Experience in e-commerce, logistics, or related industries
  • Strong analytical and problem-solving skills
  • Excellent communication and teamwork abilities
