Unacademy

About Company: Unacademy is an Indian online education platform that provides educational content such as live classes, video lectures, and practice tests for various competitive exams.

CEO: Gaurav Munjal

Headquarters: Bangalore, Karnataka, India

Founded: 2015

Industry: E-learning, Education Technology

Eligibility Criteria

  • Bachelor’s or Master’s degree in Computer Science, IT, Education, or related fields
  • Proficiency in programming languages such as JavaScript, Python, or Java
  • Experience with web development frameworks and tools
  • Passion for education and technology

Placement Process

  1. Online Application
  2. Technical Assessment
  3. Technical Interviews (Coding, System Design)
  4. HR Interview (Behavioral and Cultural Fit)
  5. Final Offer

Hiring Exam

Unacademy conducts technical assessments focusing on coding skills, data structures, algorithms, and problem-solving abilities.
