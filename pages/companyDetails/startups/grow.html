Groww

About Company: Groww is an Indian online investment platform that allows users to invest in mutual funds, stocks, ETFs, and more. It aims to make investing simple and accessible to everyone.

CEO: Lalit Keshre

Headquarters: Bangalore, Karnataka, India

Founded: 2016

Industry: Financial Services, Investment

Eligibility Criteria

  • Bachelor’s or Master’s degree in Computer Science, IT, Finance, or related fields
  • Proficiency in programming languages such as Java, Python, or JavaScript
  • Strong understanding of financial markets and investment products
  • Excellent problem-solving and analytical skills

Placement Process

  1. Online Application
  2. Technical Assessment
  3. Technical Interviews (Coding, System Design)
  4. HR Interview (Behavioral and Cultural Fit)
  5. Final Offer

Hiring Exam

Groww conducts technical assessments focusing on coding skills, data structures, algorithms, and financial knowledge.
