CRED

About Company: CRED is an Indian fintech company that rewards users for paying their credit card bills on time. The platform offers exclusive rewards and experiences to its members.

CEO: Kunal Shah

Headquarters: Bangalore, Karnataka, India

Founded: 2018

Industry: Financial Services, Fintech

Eligibility Criteria

  • Bachelor’s or Master’s degree in Computer Science, IT, Finance, or related fields
  • Proficiency in programming languages such as Python, Java, or Go
  • Strong understanding of financial technologies and credit systems
  • Excellent problem-solving and analytical skills

Placement Process

  1. Online Application
  2. Technical Assessment
  3. Technical Interviews (Coding, System Design)
  4. HR Interview (Behavioral and Cultural Fit)
  5. Final Offer

Hiring Exam

CRED conducts technical assessments focusing on coding skills, data structures, algorithms, and financial knowledge.
