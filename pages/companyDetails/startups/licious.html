Licious

About Company: Licious is an Indian meat and seafood delivery company that offers fresh and hygienic products to consumers. The platform ensures quality and timely delivery.

CEO: Abhay Hanjura & Vivek Gupta

Headquarters: Bangalore, Karnataka, India

Founded: 2015

Industry: Food Delivery, E-commerce

Eligibility Criteria

  • Bachelor’s or Master’s degree in Business, Supply Chain Management, or related fields
  • Experience in logistics, operations, or e-commerce platforms
  • Strong analytical and problem-solving skills
  • Excellent communication and teamwork abilities

Placement Process

  1. Online Application
  2. Aptitude Test
  3. Technical Interviews (Operations, Supply Chain Management)
  4. HR Interview (Behavioral and Cultural Fit)
  5. Final Offer

Hiring Exam

Licious conducts assessments focusing on operations management, supply chain logistics, and problem-solving abilities.
