BigBasket

About Company: BigBasket is India's largest online grocery delivery service, offering a wide range of products including fresh fruits and vegetables, groceries, and household essentials. Founded in 2011, BigBasket has revolutionized the way Indians shop for groceries, providing convenient home delivery options.

CEO: Hari Menon

Headquarters: Bangalore, Karnataka, India

Founded: 2011

Industry: E-commerce, Online Grocery

Number of Employees: Approximately 10,000+

Funding: BigBasket has raised substantial funding and is backed by the Tata Group.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Bachelor’s or Master’s degree in Business, Supply Chain Management, Computer Science, or related fields
  • Experience in e-commerce, retail, or supply chain management
  • Strong analytical and problem-solving skills
  • Excellent communication and teamwork abilities

Placement Process

  1. Online Application
  2. Aptitude Test
  3. Technical Interviews (Role-specific skills and knowledge)
  4. HR Interview (Behavioral and Cultural Fit)
  5. Final Offer

Hiring Exam

BigBasket conducts assessments focusing on domain knowledge, analytical abilities, and problem-solving skills relevant to the position applied for.
