Flipkart

About Company: Flipkart is one of India's leading e-commerce platforms, specializing in online retail, logistics, and digital payments.

CEO: Kalyan Krishnamurthy

Headquarters: Bangalore, India

Founded: 2007

Industry: E-commerce, Logistics, Digital Payments

Eligibility Criteria

  • Bachelor’s or Master’s degree in Computer Science or related fields
  • Strong coding skills in Java, Python, or C++
  • Knowledge of data structures, algorithms, and system design
  • Experience with cloud computing (AWS, Azure) is a plus

Placement Process

  1. Online Coding Challenge
  2. Technical Interviews (DSA, System Design, Algorithms)
  3. HR Interview (Behavioral and situational questions)
  4. Final Offer

Hiring Exam

Flipkart conducts Flipkart GRiD Challenge and Flipkart Hiring Test for technical roles.
