Salesforce

About Company: Salesforce is a cloud-based software company specializing in customer relationship management (CRM) services and enterprise applications focused on sales, customer service, and analytics.

CEO: Marc Benioff

Headquarters: San Francisco, California, USA

Founded: 1999

Industry: Cloud Computing, CRM

Eligibility Criteria

  • Bachelor’s or Master’s degree in Computer Science or related fields
  • Proficiency in programming languages such as Java, JavaScript, or Python
  • Understanding of cloud computing and SaaS architecture
  • Strong problem-solving and analytical skills

Placement Process

  1. Online Coding Assessment
  2. Technical Interviews (Covering data structures, system design, and algorithms)
  3. Behavioral Interview (Leadership and problem-solving scenarios)
  4. Final Offer

Hiring Exam

Salesforce conducts technical assessments focusing on cloud computing, CRM, and programming concepts.
