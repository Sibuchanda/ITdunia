LinkedIn

About Company: LinkedIn is a business and employment-focused social media platform that operates via websites and mobile apps. It is primarily used for professional networking and career development.

CEO: Ryan Roslansky

Headquarters: Sunnyvale, California, USA

Founded: 2002

Industry: Social Media, Technology, Professional Networking

Eligibility Criteria

  • Bachelor’s or Master’s degree in Computer Science, IT, or related fields
  • Experience with Java, Python, JavaScript, or C++
  • Understanding of scalable systems, distributed computing, and AI/ML
  • Strong problem-solving and analytical skills

Placement Process

  1. Online Technical Assessment
  2. Technical Interviews (Data Structures, Algorithms, System Design)
  3. HR Interview (Culture Fit, Leadership Skills)
  4. Final Offer

Hiring Exam

LinkedIn conducts LinkedIn Engineering Challenge and LinkedIn Coding Tests for hiring engineers.
