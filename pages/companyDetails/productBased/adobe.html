Adobe

About Company: Adobe is a global leader in digital media and digital marketing solutions, known for its products like Photoshop, Illustrator, and Adobe Experience Cloud.

CEO: Shantanu Narayen

Headquarters: San Jose, California, USA

Founded: 1982

Industry: Software Development, Digital Media

Eligibility Criteria

  • Bachelor’s or Master’s degree in Computer Science, IT, or related fields
  • Proficiency in Java, Python, C++, or JavaScript
  • Strong understanding of software development and algorithms
  • Experience with UI/UX design or cloud computing is a plus

Placement Process

  1. Online Coding Assessment
  2. Technical Interviews (Data Structures, Algorithms, System Design)
  3. Behavioral Interview (Cultural fit and communication skills)
  4. Final Offer

Hiring Exam

Adobe conducts Adobe Hiring Challenge to assess candidates on programming, problem-solving, and UI/UX skills.
