Walmart

About Company: Walmart is a multinational retail corporation that operates a chain of hypermarkets, discount department stores, and grocery stores. It also has a significant presence in e-commerce and technology-driven retail solutions.

CEO: Doug McMillon

Headquarters: Bentonville, Arkansas, USA

Founded: 1962

Industry: Retail, E-commerce, Supply Chain

Eligibility Criteria

  • Bachelor’s or Master’s degree in Computer Science, IT, or related fields
  • Proficiency in programming languages such as Java, Python, or JavaScript
  • Knowledge of data structures, algorithms, and cloud computing
  • Understanding of e-commerce and supply chain technologies

Placement Process

  1. Online Coding Challenge
  2. Technical Interviews (Data Structures, Algorithms, System Design)
  3. HR Interview (Behavioral and Leadership Assessment)
  4. Final Offer

Hiring Exam

Walmart conducts Walmart CodeHers and Walmart Global Tech Hiring Challenge for technical roles.
