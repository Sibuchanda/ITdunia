Uber

About Company: Uber is a ride-hailing, food delivery, and logistics company that operates in multiple countries, utilizing **AI and big data for transportation optimization**.

CEO: Dara Khosrowshahi

Headquarters: San Francisco, California, USA

Founded: 2009

Industry: Transportation, Technology, AI

Eligibility Criteria

  • Bachelor’s or Master’s degree in Computer Science, IT, or related fields
  • Experience in AI, ML, or distributed systems
  • Proficiency in Python, Java, C++, or Go
  • Understanding of cloud computing and microservices

Placement Process

  1. Online Technical Coding Test
  2. System Design and AI/ML Interview
  3. Behavioral and Leadership Interview
  4. Final Offer

Hiring Exam
