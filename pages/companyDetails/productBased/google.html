Google

About Company: Google is a multinational technology company specializing in internet-related services, including search engines, online advertising, cloud computing, artificial intelligence, and hardware.

CEO: Sundar Pichai

Headquarters: Mountain View, California, USA

Founded: 1998

Industry: Technology, Internet Services

Eligibility Criteria

  • Bachelor's or Master's degree in Computer Science or related fields
  • Strong foundation in data structures, algorithms, and software design
  • Proficiency in programming languages such as Python, Java, or C++
  • Excellent problem-solving and analytical skills

Placement Process

  1. Online Assessment (Coding challenges focusing on algorithms and data structures)
  2. Technical Interviews (Multiple rounds covering coding, system design, and problem-solving)
  3. Behavioral Interview (Assessment of cultural fit and communication skills)
  4. Final Offer

Hiring Exam

Google conducts technical assessments that evaluate candidates' coding abilities, problem-solving skills, and understanding of computer science fundamentals.
