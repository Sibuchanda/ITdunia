Amazon

About Company: Amazon is a global e-commerce and technology company offering online retail, cloud computing services, digital streaming, and artificial intelligence solutions.

CEO: Andy Jassy

Headquarters: Seattle, Washington, USA

Founded: 1994

Industry: E-commerce, Cloud Computing, AI

Eligibility Criteria

  • Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Engineering, or related fields
  • Strong knowledge of data structures, algorithms, and object-oriented programming
  • Proficiency in languages like Java, C++, or Python
  • Strong problem-solving and analytical abilities

Placement Process

  1. Online Assessment (Coding tests focusing on algorithms and data structures)
  2. Technical Interviews (Multiple rounds covering coding, system design, and problem-solving)
  3. Behavioral Interview (Evaluation based on Amazon's leadership principles)
  4. Final Offer

Hiring Exam

Amazon conducts online assessments that include coding challenges and behavioral questions aligned with their leadership principles.
