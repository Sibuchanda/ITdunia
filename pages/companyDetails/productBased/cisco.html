Cisco

About Company: Cisco is a global technology leader specializing in networking, security, cloud computing, and IoT solutions. It is known for its **networking hardware, software, and cybersecurity solutions**.

CEO: Chuck Robbins

Headquarters: San Jose, California, USA

Founded: 1984

Industry: Networking, Cloud Computing, Cybersecurity

Eligibility Criteria

  • Bachelor’s or Master’s degree in Computer Science, IT, Electronics, or related fields
  • Proficiency in networking concepts (CCNA, CCNP is a plus)
  • Experience in programming (Python, C++, Java) and cloud computing
  • Strong problem-solving and analytical skills

Placement Process

  1. Online Technical Assessment (Networking, Coding, Aptitude)
  2. Technical Interviews (Covering Network Security, System Design, and Problem Solving)
  3. HR Interview (Behavioral and Leadership Assessment)
  4. Final Offer

Hiring Exam

Cisco conducts **Cisco Hack Challenge** and **Cisco Hiring Test** to assess candidates in networking, cybersecurity, and cloud computing.
